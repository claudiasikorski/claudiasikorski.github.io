Meet Claudia Sikorski!

I am currently a candidate in the School of Public Health and Health Systems in my third year. Since my teenage years, I have been a strong believer in the early prevention of illness, not merely the treatment of illness! My experience at Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre in Toronto with the Cancer Genetics and High Risk Program and my time at Hamilton General Hospital as a research assistant in the Heart Investigation Unit, has only strengthened this belief. To learn more about my past experiences, check out my curricula vitæ on this site or Download my resume directly.

What will you find on this Blog?

This blog serves as a collection of original posts on several topics relating to health and disease statistics, and basics on how to use R Programming language to analyze health data (I'm still learning) through examples. You'll also find a healthy mix of recipes, my trips in nature and any other random topics I feel like sharing! A work in progress that I hope you'll enjoy!
